Spinach, Tofu & Sesame Stir-Fry

By: Martha Rose Shulman

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cut in small dice
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 16 oz baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Tip: You can prep the ingredients ahead, but cook the dish last-minute for best flavor.

Preparation

Step 1

Heat canola oil in a skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add tofu and stir-fry until golden, 3–5 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, and chili flakes, cooking until fragrant. Add soy sauce to taste. Add spinach and stir-fry until wilted. Mix in sesame seeds, adjust soy sauce, and remove from heat.

Step 2

Transfer the mixture using tongs, leaving excess liquid behind. Drizzle with sesame oil and adjust seasoning. Serve with rice, noodles, grains, or use as a pita filling.
